Introduction
My research currently focuses on how people collaboration remotely. However, topics of interest include but are limited to Remote Collaboration, Social Virtual Reality, Multimodal Communication, and Perception in Virtual Environment.
Students are strongly encouraged to submit their research to top conference and journal.
Required qualifications
  • Able to speak fluent English or Japanese
  • A strong interest in human-computer interaction
Preferred qualifications
  • Experience conducting human experiment
  • Programming experience in programming language including, but not limited to C#, python, javascript
  • Background in experimental psychology, human-computer interaction, mixed reality, or machine learning/AI
  • Knowledge about statistical data analysis
For student who is interested in applying JAIST and joining our lab
